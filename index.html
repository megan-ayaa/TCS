Who We Are

**Tackle Sickle Cell Africa (TSCA)** is a dedicated, youth-led public health initiative committed to improving the quality of life for individuals affected by sickle cell disease across Northern Uganda.

We focus on addressing critical gaps in **awareness, timely diagnosis, and comprehensive emotional support**. Our work extends beyond medical outreach to encompass community education and empowerment, ensuring sustainable impact in vulnerable areas.

Key Programs & Impact

Early Screening Initiative

Conducting **free, widespread community testing** for early diagnosis, enabling immediate intervention and genetic counseling for families.

Therapeutic Arts Program

Offering **creative and therapeutic sessions** to help warriors cope with chronic pain, manage stress, and boost emotional well-being.

Crisis Prevention & Relief

Distributing essential resources, including treated **mosquito nets** and basic medication, to mitigate common triggers like malaria and infection.

Warrior and Family Support

Providing regular hospital and home visits for psychological, social, and practical support, fostering a strong network for families.
